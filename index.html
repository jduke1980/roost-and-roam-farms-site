Small Farm • Local Goods

Simple farm goods with Southern roots.

Roost & Roam Farms is a small family farm in Washington, Georgia focused on quality, community, and honest local products.

We raise free range chickens for fresh eggs, offer freeze-dried fruits and vegetables sourced from Georgia farms, and make fun freeze-dried candy treats.

Our orchard is growing too. In the years ahead, we look forward to offering seasonal fruit and orchard-made goods from our own trees.
